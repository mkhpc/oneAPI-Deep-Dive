## Title
 Introduction to oneAPI and DPC++
  
## Requirements
| Optimized for                       | Description
|:---                               |:---
| OS                                | Linux* Ubuntu 18.04, 20 Windows* 10
| Hardware                          | Skylake with GEN9 or newer
| Software                          | Intel&reg; oneAPI DPC++ Compiler, Jupyter Notebooks, Intel Devcloud
  
## Purpose
The initial hands-on exercises in this notebook introduce you to DPC++ and the goal of oneAPI. Also, it familiarizes you with the use of Jupyter notebooks as a front-end for all training exercises. This workshop is designed to be used on the Devcloud and includes details on submitting batch jobs on the Devcloud environment.
